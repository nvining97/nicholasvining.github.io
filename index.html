Pokemon Odyssey

This is Pokemon Odyssey. A remake of Pokemon containg several of the Pokemon regions created with textures from Pokemon Platinum/Diamond/Pearl. I decided to rebuild Pokemon in java and build the code the entire engine myself. To do this I am using LibGDX, a gaming framework library.

About

The is a complete rebuild of several pokemon games written in Java and utilizing the LibGDX framework. This project was started in November of 2018.

Story

The game focuses on the main story from the original game, Pokemon Red/Blue, whith Team Rocket as the antagonist, and your goal to become the pokemon league champion. For every region I add, I will also add that regions corresponding antagonist group. storyline, and characters. I also hope to add special episodes with more story related exploration and battles that enrich the story further.

Features

Multiple regions

The game will feature multipke regions, and will hopefully include them all!

A new Quest System!

There will be a quest book that will track your progress towards you next goal! Goals will include things like beating gyms, defeating the main antagonist group, and completing side quests throughout your adventure!

Gen 5 Repel system

The repel in this game will feature repel that works the same as generation 5's, where it asks to be reapplied when it wears off.

Gen 5 EXP. system

All pokemon in your party can gain experience from a battle. Just turn on the exp. shae in your bag to enable it or keep it disabled to allow for more challenge.

All 807 current Pokemon, including Mega evolutions!

The game will allow you to capture all current 807 pokemon!

Day and Night system

You'll be able to explore the world in the day and in the night and see how the areas change accordingly!

Catch all legendaries and mythical Pokemon!

All Pokemon can caught without special events or requiring trading. Some will be gotten through events or by progressing the story.

Reusable TM's

Technical machines have unlimited uses and no longer run out.

No trade evolutions

No more trading to evolve! Use either a stone or level up a pokemon to a certain level to evolve it.

New areas and expanded original areas!

Orignal areas have been expanded and offer more area to explore while brand new areas have been introduced to provide new adventures!
